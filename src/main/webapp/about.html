SiteMe

SiteMe is an Indian photo and video sharing social networking service owned by Ishaan, created by Sumit Anand and Harish Prasad and originally launched on iOS in October 2020.

In a nutshell, SiteMe is a photo sharing app which allows users to assign filters to photos and share them with followers. ... folks can sync their photo sharing to other social networks, so SiteMe photos can be posted directly to Facebook, Tumblr, Twitter, Flickr, and Foursquare.

SiteMe is a mobile photo-sharing app, but it is also a social network. It's like Twitter with followers, only instead of real-time text updates, you provide photo updates. The app enables you to alter the photos you take with your phone by adding filtered layers that imitate the look of low-end film cameras.
